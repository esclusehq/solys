## Copywriting Contract

Phase 89 is a **copy audit**, not a copywriting phase. No new copy is being written. The contract below defines the **audit framework** for evaluating existing copy.

### Audit Categorization Framework

All existing copy and CTA text shall be classified into three risk tiers:

| Category | Label | Criteria |
|----------|-------|----------|
| 🟢 **KATA/CTA YANG AMAN** | Safe | Legally permissible without conditions. Does not contain misleading claims, unsubstantiated guarantees, trademark/copyright violations, or regulated terminology. |
| 🟡 **KATA/CTA BERISIKO** | Risky | Legally permissible but requires disclaimers, footnotes, or legal caveats. May imply warranties, service levels, or capabilities that need qualification. |
| 🔴 **KATA/CTA YANG HARUS DIHINDARI** | Must Avoid | Potentially illegal (violates consumer protection law, trademark/copyright law, or industry regulations). Misleading, deceptive, or infringing on third-party rights. |

### Audit Deliverable Structure

The report shall be grouped by the menu taxonomy specified in ROADMAP.md:

#### Product
- Features (Hero section + Features section copy)
- Pricing (plan names, pricing claims, feature descriptions, CTAs)
- Supported Games (game names, trademark references, badges)
- Changelog (version descriptions, technical claims)

#### Resources
- Documentation (external: docs.esluce.com — copy on live site)
- API Reference (external/app — copy on live site/app)
- Community Forum (external: Discord link)

#### Company
- About Us (mission, founder note, company claims)
- Legal (ToS, Privacy, License copy already on Legal.tsx, TermsOfService.tsx, PrivacyPolicy.tsx)
- Contact (contact info, response time claims)

### Specific Legal Risk Areas to Examine

| Risk Area | Pages/Sections to Scrutinize | Typical Issues |
|-----------|------------------------------|----------------|
| **Game trademark references** | Supported Games: "Minecraft", "Rust", "Terraria" | Unauthorized use of game trademarks; implying official partnership |
| **Service guarantees** | Hero: "Make Game Infrastructure Effortless"; Features: "Instant Setup" | Unsubstantiated performance claims; violation of UU ITE Pasal 28 (Indonesia) |
| **Pricing claims** | Pricing: "Simple, Transparent Pricing", "no hidden costs" | Misleading advertising; completeness of disclosure |
| **Open source licensing** | Legal: "Infrastructure configurations are available under AGPLv3" | License accuracy; AGPLv3 obligations |
| **Company/entity claims** | About Us: "a product of Resonance Systems"; Footer: "Resonance Systems" | Business entity registration status; PT/CV disclosure if applicable |
| **Data privacy promises** | Privacy Policy: "We do not sell your personal data" | Compliance with UU PDP (Indonesia Personal Data Protection Law) |
| **Liability disclaimers** | Terms: "not responsible for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages" | Enforceability under Indonesian consumer protection law |
| **Service availability claims** | Terms: "strives to maintain high availability but does not guarantee 100% uptime" | Adequacy of SLA language |
| **"Coming Soon" claims** | Supported Games: "Coming Soon" badges on Rust, Terraria | Timing claims; consumer expectation management |
| **"EARLY ACCESS" badge** | Navbar: "EARLY ACCESS" badge | Regulatory requirements for beta/early access products |
| **CTA clarity** | "Get Started", "View Documentation", "Log in", "Subscribe" buttons | Deceptive or ambiguous CTAs; automatic subscription consent |
