## Copywriting Contract

Phase 89 is a **copy audit**, not a copywriting phase. No new copy is being written. The contract below defines the **audit framework** for evaluating existing copy.

### Audit Categorization Framework

All existing copy and CTA text shall be classified into three risk tiers using a **hybrid legal framework** (Indonesian law — UU ITE, UU PDP, Consumer Protection + International — GDPR, US FTC guidelines) per D-01:

| Category | Label | Criteria | Requirements per D-02 & D-03 |
|----------|-------|----------|------------------------------|
| 🟢 **KATA/CTA YANG AMAN** | Safe | Legally permissible without conditions. Does not contain misleading claims, unsubstantiated guarantees, trademark/copyright violations, or regulated terminology. | Optional: brief note confirming compliance basis |
| 🟡 **KATA/CTA BERISIKO** | Risky | Legally permissible but requires disclaimers, footnotes, or legal caveats. May imply warranties, service levels, or capabilities that need qualification. | **Required:** Cite specific legal provisions (UU PDP articles, UU ITE pasal, GDPR articles, FTC guidance). Assess business risk per finding: enforcement likelihood, reputational impact, platform policy implications, risk severity. Provide diagnosis + konkret fix suggestions (alternative phrasings yang legally safe). |
| 🔴 **KATA/CTA YANG HARUS DIHINDARI** | Must Avoid | Potentially illegal (violates consumer protection law, trademark/copyright law, or industry regulations). Misleading, deceptive, or infringing on third-party rights. | **Required:** Cite specific legal provisions. Assess business risk per finding. Provide diagnosis + konkret fix suggestions (alternative phrasings yang legally safe). |

### Disclaimer (per D-05)

> ⚠️ **DISCLAIMER:** Laporan ini merupakan compliance review yang bersifat informatif dan bukan merupakan legal advice. Untuk keputusan hukum yang mengikat, konsultasikan dengan praktisi hukum berkompeten di yurisdiksi yang relevan.

Disclaimer ini HARUS disertakan secara prominent di awal laporan audit.

### Per-Item Output Template

For each finding in Risky and Must Avoid tiers, the report entry MUST include (per D-02, D-03):

```
### {Copy element name} — {Risk Tier}
**File:** `{path}:{line}`
**Current text:** "{exact copy}"
**Legal provisions:** {cited articles/pasal/sections}
**Risk assessment:**
  - Enforcement likelihood: {Low/Medium/High}
  - Reputational impact: {Low/Medium/High}
  - Platform policy implications: {description}
  - Risk severity: {Low/Medium/High/Critical}
**Diagnosis:** {what makes this legally problematic under the hybrid framework}
**Fix suggestion:** {concrete alternative phrasing that is legally safe}
```

## Audit Deliverable Structure

The report SHALL be structured as follows (per D-04, D-06):

### 1. Disclaimer
Prominent disclaimer that this is a compliance review, not legal advice (per D-05).

### 2. Executive Summary
Brief overview of findings per risk tier (counts per tier).

### 3. Detailed Findings by Risk Tier
Organized by risk tier (Safe → Risky → Must Avoid), **within each tier grouped by menu section** (Product, Resources, Company) per D-04.

#### 🟢 Safe Items
#### 🟡 Risky Items
#### 🔴 Must Avoid Items

### 4. Special Notes: Game Names (per D-07)
Game names (Minecraft, Rust, Terraria) → **Must Avoid** classification with fix suggestions:
- "Minecraft-compatible servers" or similar alternative phrasing
- Explicit disclaimer about no affiliation with trademark owners

### 5. Special Notes: Pricing Claims (per D-08)
"Simple, Transparent Pricing", "no hidden costs", "Free" plan → **Low priority.** Standard marketing scrutiny only. Deep analysis is skipped per D-08.

### 6. Future Recommendations (per D-06)
**Separate section** (not part of risk classification) listing recommendations for the future copy rewrite phase. These are strategic, not risk-based.

## Specific Legal Risk Areas to Examine

| Risk Area | Pages/Sections to Scrutinize | Typical Issues | Priority (per D-08) |
|-----------|------------------------------|----------------|---------------------|
| **Game trademark references** | Supported Games: "Minecraft", "Rust", "Terraria" | Unauthorized use of game trademarks; implying official partnership. **Must classify as Must Avoid with fix suggestions** (per D-07) | **HIGH** |
| **Service guarantees** | Hero: "Make Game Infrastructure Effortless"; Features: "Instant Setup" | Unsubstantiated performance claims; violation of UU ITE Pasal 28 (Indonesia) | HIGH |
| **Pricing claims** | Pricing: "Simple, Transparent Pricing", "no hidden costs" | Misleading advertising; completeness of disclosure | **LOW** — standard marketing scrutiny only (per D-08) |
| **Open source licensing** | Legal: "Infrastructure configurations are available under AGPLv3" | License accuracy; AGPLv3 obligations | MEDIUM |
| **Company/entity claims** | About Us: "a product of Resonance Systems"; Footer: "Resonance Systems" | Business entity registration status; PT/CV disclosure if applicable | MEDIUM |
| **Data privacy promises** | Privacy Policy: "We do not sell your personal data" | Compliance with UU PDP (Indonesia Personal Data Protection Law) | HIGH |
| **Liability disclaimers** | Terms: "not responsible for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages" | Enforceability under Indonesian consumer protection law | MEDIUM |
| **Service availability claims** | Terms: "strives to maintain high availability but does not guarantee 100% uptime" | Adequacy of SLA language | MEDIUM |
| **"Coming Soon" claims** | Supported Games: "Coming Soon" badges on Rust, Terraria | Timing claims; consumer expectation management | MEDIUM |
| **"EARLY ACCESS" badge** | Navbar: "EARLY ACCESS" badge | Regulatory requirements for beta/early access products | MEDIUM |
| **CTA clarity** | "Get Started", "View Documentation", "Log in", "Subscribe" buttons | Deceptive or ambiguous CTAs; automatic subscription consent | MEDIUM |

## Report Principles

1. **Risk-only report** (per D-10): Laporan bersifat klasifikasi + diagnosis + fix suggestions. Tidak ada rewrite full copy — rewrite direncanakan untuk fase terpisah.
2. **Hybrid legal framework** (per D-01): Indonesian law + GDPR + FTC. Tidak terbatas pada satu yurisdiksi.
3. **Specific citations** (per D-02): Setiap temuan Risky/Must Avoid harus menyertakan pasal/artikel spesifik.
4. **Actionable** (per D-03): Setiap temuan Risky/Must Avoid harus menyertakan diagnosis dan fix suggestions konkret.
5. **Disclaimer** (per D-05): Selalu sertakan disclaimer prominent di awal.
6. **Future recommendations** (per D-06): Pisahkan dari klasifikasi risiko, di section terpisah.
